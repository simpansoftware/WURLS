WURLS

Usage

Go to {{origin }}l/?url= and then whatever your shortened ID is to go to your shortened webpage
Example: Go to {{ origin }}l?url=NZPDI and that will bring you to https://google.com
Go to {{origin }}g/?url= and then whatever your URL is to get the shortened link for your URL
Example: Go to {{ origin }}g?url=https://google.com and that will show you the shortened link
